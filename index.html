Our Vision

We believe in bringing communities closer through technology, and empowering anyone to offer or request help instantly. Here2Help combines compassion, accessibility, and IoT innovation to make everyday life safer and more connected.

🛒 Groceries

After knee surgery, Ilana realizes she’s out of milk. She posts a quick request in the app, and Tali, who lives just two streets away, picks it up on her way home.

A small gesture - but it makes Ilana feel cared.

👴 Elderly Assistance

Danny, 82, has been sitting in a dim kitchen for days because the light bulb is too high to change safely. He posts for help, and within minutes Yosef from next door knocks on his door, replaces the bulb, and stays for a warm cup of tea and conversation.

🐕‍🦺 Dog Walking

David is stuck at work late, worried about his dog Luna waiting at home. He sends out a request, and Yael, a fellow dog lover from the building, happily takes Luna for a long walk. When David returns, Luna is calm, happy, and wagging her tail.

Key Features

Interactive Map

Visualize and explore requests in real time with an intuitive, map-based interface.

Voice & Text Requests

Submit requests effortlessly using either voice input or traditional text.

AI-Powered Categorization, Tagging & Translation

Automatically organize content and translate in real time using advanced AI.

Gamification Elements

Engage users through leaderboards, ratings, and achievement-driven interactions.

Real-Time Chat

Message helpers instantly to share details and coordinate support on the spot.

Advanced Filtering

Filter requests by distance, urgency, and category to find what matters most—fast.

QR Code Smart Sharing

Generate a scannable QR for any request to share instantly—post on doorways, flyers, or group chats for frictionless access.

Admin Console & Stats

Moderate requests, manage users, and monitor live KPIs with dashboards: trends, heatmaps, categories, and SLA-style response times.

Our Services & APIs

Built using cloud services and APIs for seamless performance.

React Native & JavaScript

Cross-platform mobile development

Firebase

Realtime database and authentication for scalable backend services.

Google & Azure Maps

Interactive geolocation and mapping for live request tracking.

Azure Blob Storage

Efficient file storage in the cloud.

Azure Speech Service

Transforms voice into text, and text into natural, lifelike speech.

Azure OpenAI

Delivers smart tagging, content categorization, and real-time translation with AI.

CountriesNow API

Provides country and city data for accurate location inputs.
