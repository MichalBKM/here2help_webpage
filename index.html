Main features

Here2Help combines community, compassion, and instant support with cutting-edge IoT and Azure cloud services.

🎤

Voice & Text Requests

Open a help request by speaking or typing. Speech-to-text enables hands-free reporting for accessibility.

🤖

Smart Categorization

Azure Text Analytics and image metadata are used to auto-classify requests and extract action items.

🗺️

Interactive Map

See open requests nearby, filter by distance and urgency, and respond with one tap. Powered by Azure Maps.

⭐

Trust & Ratings

After help is completed, users can rate helpers — building trust and rewarding active contributors with gamified badges.

Real-life scenarios

Examples from campus life that Here2Help is built to solve.

Out of diapers at night

A parent at home realizes there's no diapers for the newborn. They open a quick voice request — a neighbor brings what’s needed in minutes.

Car trouble on the roadside

A driver records a voice call for help. Helpers nearby receive the alert, one arrives with jumper cables and helps restart the car.

Printer jam before deadline

A student needs urgent help with a printer. A nearby volunteer skilled in repairs marks “I’m on my way” and resolves the issue quickly.
